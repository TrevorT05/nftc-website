Night For The Cure
NFC is a night of fun, food and fellowship where MVA Families come together to support each other in the battle against cancer. It is our collective Commitment to Courage that can have a positive impact on our community!

Gather your teammates, your classmates, your family, and friends to sponsor a Team Booth! Determine a fundraiser at Night for the Cure and purchase or acquire the items/supplies you will need. Some ideas include, but are not limited to the following: authentic ethnic food, baked goods, pizza, interactive games, sports challenges, glow sticks, etc.
